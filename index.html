July 02, 2023 - Update 0.1.010
And here we have the first minor patch to fix everything reported in the first day of testing! Nothing really game breaking, just minor annoyances really.
Any cosmetic or convenience improvements will come in the next big update. Thanks for the suggestions!

Go to the Download Page and get Version 0.1.010 right now!

To update the game, just delete the old version and play using the new one.
Your savefile is stored somewhere else, you will not lose anything.

- FIXED: Bug that allowed the player to use the same Equip Spell multiple times or even in more than one monster during the animation.
- FIXED: Problem that would stop the Duel if the NPC used "Mage Power" without a target monster.
- FIXED: Cyber-Stein crashing the Game when transforming into a Normal Monster.
- FIXED: Some issues related to the Music playing twice, overlapping or not playing at all.
- FIXED: Improved the checks for Winning via Deck Out or Burn Effects.
- And a couple of improvements on the database, general game performance and card effect descriptions.
