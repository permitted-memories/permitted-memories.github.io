July 01, 2023 - Version 0.1.000 Release!
After about 2 years in the making (with a veeeeery bad demo released back in December 2021) I'm happy to finally release a version of this game that I can be proud of!

You can click here to download Version 0.1.000 right now and give it a try!

THIS IS A BETA TESTING VERSION. Expect things to break!
Please report all bugs and eventual errors you might find at this page.


Game available in English and Brazilian Portuguese.

What is in this Version:
- Basically everything is already working as intended for the final release.
Even though you won't be able to play the Main Campaign (I'm really hyped about the plot I've written, but it's not ready yet), Tournament Mode and Free Duels are completely functional. All the characters found in Chapter 1 of the Campaign are unlocked by default and you can farm them as much as you want lol.
- GX and 5D's planned characters are locked in this version.
So you won't be able to fight them and drop their cards. You can still buy some of those with their Passwords at the Card Shop and use them if you want.
