FUSIONS

Fusion Summoning in this game works basically the same as in the original Yu-Gi-Oh! Forbidden Memories. You're supposed to fuse cards based on their type (i.e. Dragon, Warrior, Spellcaster, etc) and expect a resulting Fusion Monster that "combines" those types into something that makes sense.

There are also "Specific Fusions" following the original Yu-Gi-Oh TCG fusions and "archetype" fusions, allowing weaker archetype monsters to turn into the archetype boss. These will not be listed bellow.
Some monsters have extra types based on their artwork/lore. These will not be listed bellow.


CARD DROPS

The card drops are way more straightforward than in the original Yu-Gi-Oh! Forbidden Memories. Anything that makes sense character-wise is in the pool for that character. If you see an NPC using the card, that card can be dropped.

Three (3) cards are given when you win a duel. These cards are based on the results of your duel. The more Stars you get, the better those cards can be. The conditions to achieve better duel results will remain secret, but if you know how to A/S Pow/Tec in Forbidden Memories you'll be fine.
