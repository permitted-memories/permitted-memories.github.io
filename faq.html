WHICH FUSIONS ARE IN THIS GAME?
All the main ones from Forbidden Memories (PS1) and a lot more!
If you know the basic fusions from Forbidden Memories you will be fine. Thunder and Dragons, Females and Rocks, Beasts and Fire, and even some of the more "specific" ones are in the game.

Most of those got one more stage after what Forbidden Memories used to offer, and all monster types have been balanced to have more fusions going up to 3000 ATK if you do them right!
If you feel some Fusion is missing contact me about it!

WHO MADE THIS GAME?
Hello, I'm Rafael from Brazil. I'm 27 years old and I'm actually not a programmer.

Yu-Gi-Oh! Permitted Memories (I know, a terrible name) is my little hobbie project. I've actually started working on it back in December 2020 when I made a Youtube Video about the original PS1 Game. That got me incredbly fascinated with the logic behind some of the systems found in the game, such as how the game figures the Fusion Results, how the NPCs choose which card to play, how they think, and so on...
Even though I'm not a programmer or have any formal education on programming, I've been messing with Scripts since around 2005 mostly because of Tibia Open Servers! Then I meddled with RPG Maker, Game Maker and finally Godot, where I decided I would REALLY learn how to program something.

And that's how this little project came to life =)
It's basically the results of 2 years of studying. So yeah, I made it all alone on purpose.
